CRYPTOCURRENCY RULES

Last Update: 01/07/2025

General Rules
  • Cryptocurrency bets allow members to predict various outcomes across time ranges for selected Cryptocurrencies.
  • Cryptocurrency bets can be processed and confirmed irrespective of price changes. This may be contrary to your “Odds Preference Setting”.
  • Settlement is governed by the stated values for each Cryptocurrency at the timestamp stated within Binance website.
  • Settlement is based on time zone UTC +7.
  • If any event disrupts the market, including the suspension of or limitation of prices being stated, the company may, in its absolute discretion, close any or all open bets, refuse any bets, cancel any orders.
  • Charts displayed within the Cryptocurrency page are for analysis only. They cannot be used as a reference for wager settlement.
  • The “Close” timer is for refence only. It may not reflect the exact time when a market will be open or closed.

Bet Types

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the result after X Minutes will be over or under the value stated.
  • Should the value be the same, then bets will be settled as a push and the stake value will be returned.
  • Results will be based on the overall price stated. Decimals do count.

Last Digit

  • Predict whether the last digit result after X Minutes will be:
    • Numbered between 0-4 or 5-9.
    • Odd or Even (Odd being 1,3,5,7,9. Even being 0,2,4,6,8)
    • Any of the individual numbers stated between 0 and 9.
  • Decimals do count in respect of the last digit stated.

    For example, if the price was $83,710.01, the last digit would be “1”.
