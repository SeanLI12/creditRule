General Rules
  • Unless stated otherwise in the individual Bet Type Rules, the company reserves the right to void wagers if a match or a specific period of a match is not played to completion.
  • If the scoring format of a match differs from what was originally indicated, all bets will be void.
  • If a match starts before schedule, only the transactions before the first point starts will be considered valid. The transactions after the first point of the match will be considered invalid. (Except in-play bet types).
  • Golden Set does not count for settlement purposes.
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the match.

Set / Game - Handicap

  • Predict who will win the match with the indicated set / game handicap applied.

Set / Game - Over / Under

  • Predict whether the number of sets / games will be over or under the indicated number.

Point Handicap

  • Predict who will win more points in a specified period after applying the indicated point handicap.

Total Points - Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points will be over or under the indicated total line in a specific period.

Player / Team Points - Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points recorded by the home or away team will be over or under the indicated line.

Set / Game Betting

  • Predict the outcome of the specified Set / Game.

Match Correct Score

  • Predict the Correct Score at Full Time of a specified match.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of games, sets or points played in a specified period will be odd or even.
